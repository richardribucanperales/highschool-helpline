Who are we?

We are the students of Waipahu High, dedicating our time to educate the students, teachers, and staff of Waipahu High School about Relationship Abuse, Sexual Violence, and Depression. Our dedicated webmasters created this website with blood, sweat, and tears to give you a better understanding of what we want the people of Waipahu to know about the sensitive topics, and provide information on how to stop the abuse.
